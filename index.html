About CyberShield AI

What is CyberShield AI?

CyberShield AI is an advanced AI-powered cybercrime assistance chatbot designed to help ordinary people understand and respond to cybercrime incidents. It uses Google Gemini AI to analyze your situation and provide detailed, actionable guidance.

Disclaimer

CyberShield AI provides general guidance only. It is NOT a substitute for legal advice or official law enforcement. Always contact 1930 or cybercrime.gov.in for official assistance. The AI may make errors — use responses as a starting point only.

Technology Stack

Frontend: HTML5 · CSS3 · Vanilla JS
AI: Google Gemini 2.0 Flash API
APIs: Web Speech API · Speech Synthesis API · localStorage
No backend · No database · Fully client-side
